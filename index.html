I am a researcher specializing in land and housing valuation and urban management. Currently, I am working at Center for Land Policy and Valuations from the Polytechnic University of Catalonia, focusing on the intersection of urban planning and management and data science.

My work aims to explore fundamental principles, seeking elegant mathematical solutions to complex problems. I am particularly interested in how theoretical frameworks can be applied to understand real-world urban phenomena.

My primary research focuses on the following areas:

  • • Urban Dynamics: Modeling valuation in complex metropolitan systems.
  • • Data Science in Planning: Applying statistical methods for large geospatial datasets.
  • • Land Policy: Investigating the economic impact of urban regulations.
